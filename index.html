02 / Data Analytics · Dec 2025 – Jan 2026
US Candy Distributor — Margin Improvement Strategy
RevoU — Capstone Project
Background

Wonka's Chocolate & Candy Distributor is a B2B wholesaler operating 15 products across Chocolate, Sugar, and "Other" divisions in the US. With market margins between 60–70%, the company needed data-driven optimization to close a 1.59pp profitability gap and maintain its competitive standing.

Method / Strategy / Solution

Integrated 6 interlinked tables from Kaggle covering 10,194 transactions (2021–2024). Performed data cleaning and exploratory analysis to surface cost inefficiencies and portfolio imbalances. Built a Tableau dashboard with 9 calculated fields for margin analysis, delivering a 3-tier action plan targeting the "Other" division as the primary root cause.

Result / Output

Identified the "Other" division (44.84% margin, costs 3.7× higher than Chocolate) as the root cause. Recommended phasing out Kazookles (+0.50pp margin boost), restructuring "Other" division cost base to under $3.00/unit, and scaling Everlasting Gobstopper. Projected to reach a 67.76% gross margin by Q4 2025.
